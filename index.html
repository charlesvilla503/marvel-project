How Does This Work?

NOTE: Marvel only allows for 100 results to be returned. Fastest results come in one month queries (i.e 11/01/1991 to 12/01/1991)

  1. Enter your date range
  2. The populated results link to their Marvel.com profiles providing you more info such as storyline, date published, and artists/writers
  3. Click the "Find Issues on eBay" button to search eBay for the corresponding issue.
  4. Click the image to go to that eBay listing
  5. If no issues are available, or are listed by a foreign seller, a pop-up will provide you with a link to the eBay query.

HAVE FUN!
